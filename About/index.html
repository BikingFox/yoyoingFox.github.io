What's with the name?

I'm sure your wondering were a name like "Slow Poke Computers" came from. Here is the quick background story. My nephew and I were working on my grampa's computer, and we were moving computers and parts between my house and his house with a wagon. When we got to his house, he laughed and called us slow poke computers. And that's were the name and logo came from.
